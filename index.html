What is OPD Coverage ?

Out-Patient Diagnosis or OPD covers the treatment received by a patient in the form of consultations and pharmaceuticals. Traditional Health Insurance is not the same as an OPD insurance.

Traditional Health Insurance (without OPD rider) covers treatment of a patient only under hospitalization.

OPD insurance covers medical consultation costs and prescribed pharmaceuticals provided by a medical practitioner in addition to hospitalization cover.
